Kevonie Bishop

TITLE HEADING

Title description, Dec 7, 2020

Kevonie Bishop is a 22-year-old multifaceted visionary born on August 18, 2001, with a heart ablaze for impacting lives through his dynamic ministry. As a preacher, worship leader, songwriter, and aspiring author, Kevonie embodies a rare fusion of talent, passion, and purpose. His mission is to inspire and equip young adults to walk in the fullness of their God-given potential, igniting a movement of faith and purpose across the globe. Through his music, preaching, and writing, Kevonie seeks to see a generation transformed, empowered to embrace their identity and destiny with unwavering faith..
