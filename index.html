My Life

Born August 18, 2001

Kevonie Bishop is a dynamic 22-year-old preacher, worship leader, songwriter, and aspiring author, born on August 18, 2001. Passionate about empowering young adults to discover and fulfill their God-given purposes, he inspires through his music, preaching, and writing. Kevonie's mission is to see a generation transformed by the power of God, walking boldly in faith and purpose..
